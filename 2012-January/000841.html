It seems 7zip can open ISO images, according to their website.
http://www.7-zip.org/
"Supported formats:
    Packing / unpacking: 7z, XZ, BZIP2, GZIP, TAR, ZIP and WIM
    Unpacking only: ARJ, CAB, CHM, CPIO, CramFS, DEB, DMG, FAT, HFS, ISO,
LZH, LZMA, MBR, MSI, NSIS, NTFS, RAR, RPM, SquashFS, UDF, VHD, WIM,
XAR and Z."

The OP seems to be saying that 7zip doesn't seem to be able to open ISO
images created with mkisofs.
This may or may not be an issue with mkisofs. It could also be a bug in 7zip.
