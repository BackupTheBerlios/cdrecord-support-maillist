So this is the win-dos version of 7-zip or does this also apply to the OSS UNIX 
variant?

with the UNIX variant I checked unpacking of tar archives some weeks ago and it 
turned out, that the TAR support is very rudimentary.

Regadring the ISO-9660, Rock Ridge and UDF, recent versions of mkisofs are 
definitely much better in standard compliance than previous versions have been.

In general: If you have repeatable problems with a specific program, make a bug 
report gainst this program - in your case this is 7-zip.

The 7-zip author should know why you have problems and in case he believes that 
is caused by a mkisofs bug, he should describe the problem. I then could have a 
look at it and tell you whether there is a bug in mkisofs or a bug / 
missinterpretation of the standard in 7-zip.
