Murray Blakeman <mblakeman at netspace.net.au> wrote:

> I recently purchased a Pioneer Bluray Writer BDR-205BKRP.  I'm trying to 
> do some testing to ensure it works correctly.
>
> I'm running Solaris 10u8 and have installed cdrtools 3.0.0.

Did you swich on DMA for this drive?
Solaris 10 was released more than 5.5 years ago and was build on outdated 
knowlegde from Solaris 9...

Pioner drives won't work correctly if there is no DMA.

eeprom
ata-dma-enabled=1
atapi-cd-dma-enabled=1

Make sure both is on, reboot after a change.

format information that is will not accept.
