JOYDEEP <j.bakshi at unlimitedmail.org> wrote:

> 1> How can I check with cdrecord/cdrtools that the drive is empty ?

Check readcd sectors=0-0

> 2> How can I check the CD-R (with some data in it) support mutisession
> or not ?

cdrecord -minfo

> 3> What is the difference between a Diskcopy than Cloning ? and what is
> the best method of cloning i.e by cdrecord or by cdread ?

cloning also copies CD sub-channel data.

How to to it is in the man pages.
