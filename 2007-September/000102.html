OK, I have done it in a different way.
I first closed the tray and then check the existance of the drive as below

===================================
cdrecord -dev=ATA:1,0,0 -load 2>/dev/null 1>/dev/null

readcd -dev=ATA:1,0,0 -sectors=0-0 -f=/dev/null 2>/dev/null

case $? in

1) ;;
255) dialog --colors --fb --title " Easy CD Writer for Linux "\
      --msgbox "\Z0 Drive is empty" 30 60
esac
