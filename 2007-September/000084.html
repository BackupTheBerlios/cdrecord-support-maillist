Looks like you own a drive that disables DMA for the read buffer command.
This is a problem of your drive.....it missinforms cdrecord about the maximum 
speed.

I own a Plextor drive and I know that this problem is present with old firmware.
It is not seen in any case if the computer is fast enough to get a sufficient 
transfer speed in PIO mode.

This is what you get with the latest "free" firmware:
