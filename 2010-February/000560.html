Sebastien TOPIN wrote:

> I understand that Joliet is not standard but to my knowledge there is  
> no Rock Ridge support on Windows platform and I need to be compatible  
> with CDs burned on Windows.

You don't need Rock Ridge support at OS level.
Mkisofs read Rock Ridge names from previous setting.

Use 'mkisofs -joliet-long -r' at first and following sessions.
