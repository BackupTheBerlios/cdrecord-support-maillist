People might have noticed that the development for XCDroast
did slow down dramatically since xcdroast-0.98alpha15
was released October 30 2003.

Since that time mainly bug fixes have been created.

The main advantage of XCDroast before many other CD/DVD/BluRay
recording GUIs is that XCDroast does not try to send SCSI commands
from the GUI and that XCDroast does not interface with hald and similar.
For this reason, XCDraost is highly portable and is able to work on all 
platforms that are supported by cdrtools:

AIX
AmigaOS
APOLLO/Domain OS
ATARI MiNT
BeOS
Haiku
Zeta
BSDi
FreeBSD/OpenBSD/NetBSD/DragonFlyBSD
DOS
HP-UX
Linux
Mac OS X
NeXT
SCO OpenServer
SCO UnixWare
OS/2
Digital UNIX
OSF-1
True64
QNX
IRIX
SunOS-4.x
SunOS-5.x (Solaris)
Syllable
VMS
Win95
Win98
WinNT (all Win32)
Interix (Win32 + Services for UNIX)
Cygwin

Another advantage is that this also makes use of all workarounds for
OS bugs and Drive Firmware bugs that are present in cdrtools.

We are looking for a person with experiences in writing GTK2 software
who is interested to prevent XCDroast from dying and who is interested
in helping to let XCDroast make use of the various new featured from cdrtools.
