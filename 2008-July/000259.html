1
2
3


BTW: if you use GNU make, failing comands will _always_ be ignored with respect 
to commands that follow in the same shell call. This is because GNU make calls:

sh -c "cmd ..."

while UNIX make and smake call:

sh -ce "cmd ..." 

Another reason why I recommend not to use gmake.
