I would asume that this delay is caused by the drives firmware.

A DVD needs to carry at least 800 MB but with DVD+R this is portion from the 
standard usually not enforced. DVD+R usually rather writes a too short 
lead-out. Your drive may behave different.

You are using a no-name drive and thus it is hard to tell anything on the 
drives estimated behavior.
