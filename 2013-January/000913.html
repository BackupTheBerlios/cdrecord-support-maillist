Another note that may help to understand the problem:

if you write less than the whole capacity of a dual layer disk, it may help to 
define a useful layer break value (see man page of cdrecord) in order to 
prevent the drive from trying to write padding to the end of the real disk 
(which may take a long time) after your data has been transferred to the disk.
