For errors like this, I recommend to kill or suspend (kill -STOP <pid>) "hald" 
or similar hostile programs before starting to write.

If you are writing a CD, you may alternatively write in -raw96r mode but this 
does not work for DVDs.

Hald and it's successors (from the same author who is unwilling to fix his 
bugs) try to access the medium while it is written and thus cause this kind of 
problems.
