Just as you said, for some reason gnome-volume-manager was called after
cdrecord finishes blanking the media and it didn't behave correctly in
SLES10, I finally got the workaround... for your reference:
1.Start "gnome-volume-properties"
2.Select the tab "Storage"
3.In the area of "Removable Storage", unselect "Mount removable media when
inserted"
May this will help~~~Thx anyway :)
