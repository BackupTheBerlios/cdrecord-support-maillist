On Thu, 10 June 2010, Eric Shattow wrote:
> Which cdrtools executables are safe to install suid root?   Is
> cdrecord the only one?
> 
> Does i.e. cdda2wav require suid root?
 
cdda2wav, readcd and cdrecord, i.e. all that have to send SCSI commands
to the drive need the sufficient rights to do so, e.g. by setting suid
root.

I would recommend to use dpkg-statoverride in
{cdrecord,cdda2wav}.postinst with a corresponding
{cdrecord,cdda2wav}.postrm.
