If you probably tell tell mkisofs to put the same names in different 
subdiretories to the same location on the CD, you should expect problems from 
dupplicate names.

If you have problems to understand the -x syntax, try the -find syntax. e.g.:

mkisofs -R -o xxx.iso -find . ! -path './subdir*'
