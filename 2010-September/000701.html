If you like to get help, you need to send informations related to your problem.

2.01.01a80 is outdated, but cdrtools-3.00 is nearly identical.

librscg did not change since more than a year and if the make call did not stop
as librscg, you are using a defective shell that does not follow POSIX standards
and the rules how UNIX shells behave since 1977.

In any case, it is most unlikely that the difference between a79 and a80 is the 
cause for your problem.
