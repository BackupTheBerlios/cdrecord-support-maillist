You don't give any information on the media, but either the media is bad, or 
your optical system collected dust.

But you ignored the manual page and started cdrecord with an unsupported 
command line.... dev=/dev/cdrom is a bad idea as Linux has many drivers for a 
single drive and you may this way force cdrecord to use the worst driver.

Use the official canonical way and e.g. simply omit dev= in case you have just 
one drive or use the official SCSI address names.
