60 DVDs! That's almost 300GB. I have been using a removable HD rack
system for backup purposes, and using DVDs for data that needs to
be duped/shared/stored at different locations. A 500GB SATA drive can be
had in the $50 range and at that price, a second backup can be justified
if the data is critical.

Also, keep an eye on the SDHC card technology. There are 64GB units, but
the price is currently in the $150 range. About every 2 years, max capacity
doubles and cost per GB halves. Problem is that requirements always seem
to grow to match. :(
